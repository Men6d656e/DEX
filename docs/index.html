Explore the world of decentralized finance through hands-on interaction with a fully functional mock exchange. No real funds required.

💰

Faucet

Mint mock ETH and BTC tokens daily with a 24-hour cooldown. Track your lifetime claims and claim history.

🔄

Swap

Trade mock tokens (mETH ↔ mUSDC) with instant ratio-based execution, slippage protection, and reserve tracking.

📊

Analytics

Real-time candlestick charts from CoinGecko, token statistics, trade history, and portfolio tracking.

Quick Start

  1. Connect your wallet — Use MetaMask or any injected wallet. Switch to Anvil (local) or Sepolia (testnet).
  2. Get mock tokens — Visit the Faucet page to claim mETH and mBTC (10 tokens each every 24h).
  3. Start trading — Head to the Swap page to trade mETH for mUSDC.
  4. Track performance — The Analytics page shows market data and your portfolio.
